about

A club for all who seek to engage with the outdoors. Our adventures take us to local spots in the city and to natural areas beyond the five boroughs. Outdoor experience not needed!

Columbia Outdoors Club was founded in 2016, with the intention to cultivate an outdoorsy community on campus. We were recognized by the Activities Board at Columbia (ABC) in 2017.
Columbia Outdoors Club, based in Morningside Heights, Manhattan is headquartered on Lenape Land. It is our responsibility to acknowledge and engage with the indigeneity of all lands we recreate on, to recognize the history of colonialism and critically question our place in recreating in American public lands.
